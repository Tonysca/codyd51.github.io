Acute. A Revolutionary Voice Recognition Assistant

Acute is a revolutionary voice recognition suite designed to make controlling your device and your world as simple as spoken word. Just set up phrases in Acute, tightly integrated with Activator, assign them to events, and just speak to your device. Once activated, Acute is always listening, so theres no need to hold the home button or do other cumbersone gestures. Once it's activated, you're ready to start using your device in a whole new way.

Hermes. Incredibly Efficient, Cross Platform Quick Reply.

Hermes is a quick reply solution enabling you to immediately reply to any Messages, Kik, or WhatsApp message you recieve. Unlike other quick reply tweaks which use proprietary alert systems, Hermes uses iOS's built-in alert system which, along with other optimzations, have earned it the titlte of the most efficient and lightweight quick reply tweak on the market, not to mention supporting the most platforms.

CamText. Always See Where You're Walking.

CamText changes your Messages app background to a camera view, so when you're too busy to put down your phone or *really* need to answer that text, you never run the rish of losing sight of where you're walking. CamText makes texting while walking both smarter and safer.

Conformity. Never Be Bothered By Calls Again.

The incoming call screen is a burden for us all. It's clunky, wholly unnecessary and obtrusive, replacing whatever you were looking at without warning. Conformity fixes this. Conformity replaces the large incoming call and in-call screens with a minimalistic, unobtrusive, draggable bar on the screen which can be manipulated to perform call actions. Never be interruped by calls again.
